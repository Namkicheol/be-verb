Point A · 20문제

be동사의 현재형과 부정문

선택형(1-10) + 주관식(11-20)
1
He ___ my teacher.
💡 정답: is — He(3인칭 단수) → is
2
They ___ my neighbors.
💡 정답: are — They(복수) → are
3
We ___ in the library.
💡 정답: are — We(복수) → are
4
I ___ 140 cm tall.
💡 정답: am — I → am
5
The movie ___ funny.
💡 정답: is — The movie(단수) → is
6
You ___ late for school.
💡 정답: are — You → are
7
Irene and Jim ___ in the same class.
💡 정답: are — 복수 주어 → are
8
This jacket ___ expensive. I can buy it easily.
💡 정답: is not — 단수 → is not
9
It ___ sunny now. It is cloudy and rainy.
💡 정답: is not — It(단수) → is not
10
My parents ___ doctors. They are teachers.
💡 정답: are not — 복수 → are not
11
Sally and her sister middle school students.
💡 정답: are — 복수 → are
12
The answer right. It is wrong.
💡 정답: is not — 단수 → is not
13
I in the soccer club.
💡 정답: am — I → am
14
These shoes pretty.
💡 정답: are — These shoes(복수) → are
15
Tom and I best friends.
💡 정답: are — Tom and I(복수) → are
16
The weather not nice today.
💡 정답: is — The weather(단수) → is
17
Jaeho from Korea.
💡 정답: is — Jaeho(3인칭 단수) → is
18
Both clubs fun!
💡 정답: are — Both clubs(복수) → are
19
I not a soccer fan. I like basketball.
💡 정답: am — I → am + not
20
Michael a teacher. He is a student.
💡 정답: is not — 단수 → is not
Point B · 20문제

일반동사의 현재형과 부정문

선택형·주관식 번갈아 구성
1
I ___ two sisters. We resemble each other.
💡 정답: have — I → 그대로 have
2
He three languages. (speak)
💡 정답: speaks — He(3인칭) → speak+s
3
Kevin ___ violin lessons on Mondays.
💡 정답: takes — Kevin(3인칭) → take+s
4
I in Incheon now. I live in Suwon. (live, 부정형)
💡 정답: don't live — I → don't+원형
5
Judy is a teacher. She ___ science.
💡 정답: teaches — She(3인칭) → teach+es
6
Christina meat. She is a vegetarian. (eat, 부정형)
💡 정답: doesn't eat — She(3인칭) → doesn't+원형
7
My parents ___ for a hospital. They are teachers.
💡 정답: don't work — They(복수) → don't+원형
8
The movie in five minutes. (start)
💡 정답: starts — 단수 → start+s
9
Mia ___ to school on foot.
💡 정답: goes — Mia(3인칭) → go+es
10
Ellie her house every day. (clean)
💡 정답: cleans — Ellie(3인칭) → clean+s
11
Sophie ___ camping with her family.
💡 정답: goes — Sophie(3인칭) → go+es
12
I soccer with my friends in my free time. (play)
💡 정답: play — I → 그대로 play
13
We ___ English every day.
💡 정답: study — We(복수) → 그대로 study
14
She healthy because she exercises every day. (feel)
💡 정답: feels — She(3인칭) → feel+s
15
Diego ___ pictures of Bella online.
💡 정답: posts — Diego(3인칭) → post+s
16
My family camping very often. (go)
💡 정답: goes — My family(단수) → go+es
17
Amara ___ dance moves every day.
💡 정답: practices — Amara(3인칭) → practice+s
18
He soccer. He likes baseball. (play, 부정형)
💡 정답: doesn't play — He(3인칭) → doesn't+원형
19
The baby often ___ at night.
💡 정답: cries — 3인칭 단수 → cry→cries
20
Sophie a baguette in the picture. She is from France! (have)
💡 정답: has — Sophie(3인칭) → have→has
